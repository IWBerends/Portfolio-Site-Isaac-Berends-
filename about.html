About

My Story

Hi, I’m Isaac Berends a Designer / Illustrator residing in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

BFA / MCAD

I started my design career at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design. There I landed an internship which transitioned into my first job as a graphic designer / product designer.

From there I expanded my horizon and found a new career path as an environmental designer. There, I learned some new skills while refining my design expertise. I branched into 3D modeling, concept illustration, technical illustration, graphic design, and production.

While working there, I took the leap into the digital world, learning more skills to transition into UI design.

I have a profound fascination with art, design, tech, architecture and the natural world. I love problem solving, and creating designs that give users an enjoyable, intuitive, and beautiful experience. I find myself to be logical and detailed oriented.

When not creating, I spend a majority of my time traveling, camping, biking, enjoying science based documentaries, film, visiting museums, cooking a delicious meal, and enjoying dinning at a fine restaurants.
